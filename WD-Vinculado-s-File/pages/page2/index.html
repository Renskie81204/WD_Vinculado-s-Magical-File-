About

riotgames.com Riot Games Riot Games, an American video game developer, publisher, and esports tournament organizer, was founded in 2006 by Brandon Beck and Marc Merrill. The company's mission was to change the way video games are developed, published, and supported for players. In 2009, Riot Games released its debut title, League of Legends, a multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game that quickly became a global phenomenon. Known for its player-focused approach, Riot Games continuously updates League of Legends and has expanded its universe with additional games, including Teamfight Tactics, Legends of Runeterra, and Valorant. Beyond game development, Riot Games has significantly impacted the esports industry, organizing and sponsoring numerous tournaments worldwide. Headquartered in Los Angeles, Riot Games operates offices globally to support its growing community of players and fans.
